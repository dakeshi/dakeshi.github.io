Maintain your HubPress with the latest version

How to maintain your HubPress with lastest version?

With cross compare fork feature of github, you can always use updated HubPress.

  1. Click Compare button in your HubPress repository

  2. Change base fork to your repository. I use the master branch here. But if you want to use another branch, you can change it. For example, it would be gh-pages branch.

  3. Select compare across forks.

  4. Change head fork to HubPress/hubpress.io repository.

Now, you can use the most recent HubPress version with bug fixed, new features.
